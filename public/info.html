How to Use UV index.

Cited EPA.gov

UV index Risk Level Action
0 to 2 Low If you burn easily, cover up and use broad spectrum SPF 30+ sunscreen.
3 to 5 Moderate Generously apply broad spectrum SPF 30+ sunscreen every 2 hours, even on cloudy days, and after swimming or sweating.
6 to 7 High Generously apply broad spectrum SPF 30+ sunscreen every 2 hours, even on cloudy days, and after swimming or sweating.
8 to 10 Very High Minimize sun exposure between 10 a.m. and 4 p.m.
11 or more Extreme Try to avoid sun exposure between 10 a.m. and 4 p.m.
